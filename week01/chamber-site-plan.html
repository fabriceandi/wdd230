Site name

TanaSoa Chamber

This name symbolized the capital of Madagascar, Antananarivo, and its potential for growth and success, reflecting the chamber's mission.

Site Purpose

The TanaSoa Chamber is dedicated to supporting local business by promoting community development, facilitating communication between businesses, providing updates on the Malagasy business environments.
